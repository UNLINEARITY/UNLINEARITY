## About Me

I previously studied Automation at **China University of Petroleum**, and I am now a graduate student working in robotics and embodied intelligence at the **Chinese Academy of Sciences**.

I am deeply interested in intelligence itself, especially systems science, automatic control, robotics, embodied intelligence, and the way knowledge can be connected into reusable structures.

## What I'm Building

I'm building a nonlinear knowledge system around **bidirectional links** and **relational graphs**, with related tools for learning, automation, robotics, and creative workflows.

- [Atlas-of-Control-and-AI](https://github.com/UNLINEARITY/Atlas-of-Control-and-AI): a knowledge atlas for mathematics, automation, control theory, computer science, and AI. Its published site is [unlinearity.top](https://www.unlinearity.top/) (currently only available in Chinese).
- [Obsidian-CodeSpace](https://github.com/UNLINEARITY/Obsidian-CodeSpace): an Obsidian plugin for viewing, managing, and editing code files.
- [CLI-WeChat-Bridge](https://github.com/UNLINEARITY/CLI-WeChat-Bridge): a bridge between AI command-line tools and WeChat-based workflows.
